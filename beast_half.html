Beast Half as Brave

Lisa Tantillo

This collection by Lisa Tantillo is based off the lines of warning in the opening of the Jaberwocky, a poem by Lewis Carroll:

"Beware the Jabberwock, my son!
The jaws that bite, the claws that catch!
Beware the Jubjub bird, and shun
The frumious Bandersnatch!"

The collection explores our complex relationship with nature--- how we are simultaneously awed by its beauty, yet frightened by the unknown.

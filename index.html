Calou Calay is an independent art venture by Lisa Tantillo and Zach Callaghan featuring handmade ceramics, sculptures, paintings and prints. Founded in 201X, our work is inspired by the fantastical imagery in the poem The Jabberwocky by Lewis Carroll.

We use our work to shine rays of compassion and positivity through the shuddering darkness. Don't let negativity devour your thoughts. Let the sun rise within.
